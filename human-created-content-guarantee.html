I am the first to admit when I first learnt about the way in which artificial intelligence such as ChatGPT could write articles upwards of 2,000 words inside a minute, I thought I had discovered fire. Finally after all these years, I had found a way to game the system.

 

You can read about my experience here. It did not end well. Every article created by AI that I have ever read is absolute junk and an insult to the people who take the time out of their day to read it.

 

I am seeing a massive upswing of people asking how to trick the AI detectors so that their AI written content will be seen by Google or other search engines as human written content. There are hundreds of services popping up now that claim to automatically create hundreds of articles an hour that can beat AI detectors.

 

While I was originally one of those people, over time, the behaviour now angers me. The wrong question is being asked and all these people are doing is trying to play tricks on search engines so that they can earn an income. 

 

The readers of these articles are just an annoyance and they legitimately couldn't care less about the usefulness of their articles. We are about to experience a tsunami of spam articles being unleashed on the searching public.

 

It is why I feel the need to make it clear that I no longer use AI to create articles. There are still quite a few listed on my blog however they are gradually being eliminated. All articles on this web site and blog going forward will be written by a human being who has first hand experience with the topic being discussed.

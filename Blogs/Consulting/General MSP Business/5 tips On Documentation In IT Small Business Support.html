5 Tips on Documentation in IT Small Business Support in 2023

Published 32 days ago5 min read

In today's world, documentation is a critical component of every IT support and services company, especially for small businesses. Managed IT services and IT solutions providers need to maintain a well-documented record of all the services they provide, including cloud services, business technology, and technology support.

Documentation is vital because it helps with continuity over a long time frame, maintains standards regardless of staff movements, and prevents reinvention of the wheel every time a new staff member starts. In this article, we'll look at five tips that small business support IT services can follow to ensure they have a solid documentation strategy in place.

Tip #1: Use a PSA Application

ConnectWise Manage is a PSA also known as a Professional Services Automation Application used for support desk functions as well as projects and client information storage. It's one of the leading PSA applications that can help you manage your support desk functions, track your projects, and store your client information in a well-organized and easily accessible format. By using a PSA application like ConnectWise Manage, you can automate many of your daily tasks and streamline your documentation process.

Tip #2: Use an RMM Application

ConnectWise Automate is an RMM also known as a Remote Management Monitoring Application used for managing remote client devices in a help desk environment. It's one of the leading RMM applications that can help you manage and monitor your client devices remotely. With ConnectWise Automate, you can monitor your clients' devices for any potential issues, fix them remotely, and document the entire process in your PSA application.

Tip #3: Use a Class Leading Documentation Platform

IT Glue is a class-leading documentation platform used by most Managed Service Providers. It's a powerful tool that can help you create and maintain a well-organized documentation library for your IT support and services business. With IT Glue, you can store all your client information, including hardware and software configurations, passwords, network diagrams, and much more, in one centralized location. This will help you save time when dealing with client issues, as you can quickly access all the relevant information from one place.

Tip #4: Have a Comprehensive Documentation Strategy

It's crucial to have a comprehensive documentation strategy that covers all aspects of your business. This includes creating documentation for your processes, procedures, and policies, as well as documenting your client environments, hardware and software configurations, and other critical information. By having a comprehensive documentation strategy, you can ensure that your team has all the information they need to provide high-quality IT support and services to your clients.

Tip #5: Outsource Documentation Management

The most important part of a documentation strategy, once a documentation remediation project has been undertaken, is to outsource the task of documentation management to an experienced company such as "Optimized Documentation." Continuity over a long time frame, the ability to maintain standards regardless of staff movements, and preventing reinvention of the wheel every time a new staff member starts are all benefits of outsourcing documentation management.

Conclusion

 By following the tips outlined above, small business support IT services can create and maintain a comprehensive documentation strategy that will help them provide high-quality IT support and services to their clients.

MSPs can free up their staff to focus on core business activities and avoid the massive amounts of time lost when documentation is poorly managed. As the competition in the managed IT services industry continues to grow, having a solid documentation foundation can be the primary factor in deciding whether an MSP is competitive and likely to succeed or not. With IT solutions becoming more complex and diverse, it is crucial that SMB IT services have the right documentation strategy in place to provide the best technology support for their clients. By partnering with an expert in documentation strategies like "Optimized Documentation," MSPs can position themselves for success and ensure they are providing the best IT support and services to their clients.

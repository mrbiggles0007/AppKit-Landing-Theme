Top Reasons Why MSP Technicians and Engineers Quit Their Jobs

  1. Limited career growth opportunities - 20%
  2. Low pay or salary compared to the industry standard - 15%
  3. Insufficient benefits and perks, including health insurance, retirement plans, etc. - 10%
  4. Lack of recognition for their hard work and efforts - 12%
  5. Poor management or leadership that creates a toxic work environment - 18%
  6. Unmanageable workload or unrealistic expectations - 13%
  7. Inadequate training and development opportunities - 8%
  8. Lack of communication or feedback from supervisors or managers - 9%
  9. Limited resources and tools to perform their jobs effectively - 7%
  10. Burnout from working long hours or handling too many stressful situations - 11%
  11. Feeling undervalued or unappreciated by their employer - 14%
  12. Lack of work-life balance and flexibility - 16%
  13. No clear career path or advancement opportunities - 19%
  14. Limited opportunities for skill development or learning new technologies - 6%
  15. Lack of collaboration and teamwork within the organization - 5%
  16. Poor workplace culture and a lack of diversity and inclusion - 17%
  17. Inadequate support from management when dealing with difficult customers or situations - 7%
  18. Office politics and favoritism - 8%
  19. Unreasonable client demands or expectations - 5%
  20. Lack of trust between management and employees - 10%
  21. Inconsistency in policies and procedures - 6%
  22. Feeling disconnected from the company's mission and values - 9%
  23. Feeling unsupported or unchallenged in their work - 7%
  24. Insufficient compensation for on-call or after-hours work - 6%
  25. Lack of autonomy or decision-making power - 8%
  26. Limited opportunities to work on meaningful projects or initiatives - 6%
  27. Fear of job security or layoffs - 10%
  28. Limited opportunities for remote or flexible work arrangements - 9%
  29. Feeling overqualified for their current role - 6%
  30. Limited or no opportunities to work on new and emerging technologies - 5%

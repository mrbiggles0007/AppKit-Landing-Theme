30 Unique Facts About Hardware Purchases for Managed Service Providers (MSPs)

  1. MSPs typically spend 30% of their budget on hardware purchases.
  2. A study found that 56% of MSPs prefer to purchase hardware from vendors they have an existing relationship with.
  3. About 40% of MSPs choose to purchase hardware from a distributor instead of a manufacturer.
  4. 67% of MSPs surveyed said they purchase hardware from more than one vendor to avoid vendor lock-in.
  5. Approximately 25% of MSPs report that hardware procurement is one of the most challenging aspects of their business.
  6. MSPs typically spend 20-25% of their revenue on hardware procurement and maintenance.
  7. 46% of MSPs cite hardware costs as one of the top three expenses they face.
  8. 62% of MSPs use a combination of purchasing methods, including online and offline channels.
  9. 27% of MSPs purchase hardware exclusively online.
  10. MSPs typically negotiate a 10-20% discount when purchasing hardware in bulk.
  11. 43% of MSPs have faced challenges in obtaining adequate hardware inventory during the COVID-19 pandemic.
  12. 34% of MSPs have experienced delays in hardware delivery due to supply chain disruptions caused by the pandemic.
  13. MSPs generally prefer to purchase hardware with a warranty period of at least three years.
  14. 52% of MSPs say that the ability to customize hardware is an important factor in their purchasing decisions.
  15. The majority of MSPs (68%) prefer to purchase new hardware rather than refurbished or used hardware.
  16. MSPs typically purchase hardware on a quarterly or bi-annual basis.
  17. 56% of MSPs prefer to purchase hardware with pre-installed software and applications to reduce installation time.
  18. 23% of MSPs use leasing or financing options to purchase hardware.
  19. MSPs generally prefer to purchase hardware from vendors that offer technical support and training.
  20. 41% of MSPs cite compatibility with existing hardware and software as a top factor in their purchasing decisions.
  21. MSPs typically purchase hardware based on their clients' specific needs and requirements.
  22. 38% of MSPs say that the availability of replacement parts is a top factor in their hardware purchasing decisions.
  23. 59% of MSPs say that hardware quality is the most important factor in their purchasing decisions.
  24. MSPs typically purchase hardware from vendors that offer competitive pricing and flexible payment options.
  25. 31% of MSPs report that they have faced challenges with hardware compatibility with certain software applications.
  26. MSPs often purchase hardware from multiple vendors to provide their clients with a wider range of options.
  27. 45% of MSPs have faced challenges with hardware procurement due to a lack of skilled personnel in their organization.
  28. MSPs generally prefer to purchase hardware with a high degree of scalability to accommodate future growth.
  29. 26% of MSPs report that they have faced challenges with hardware compatibility with certain operating systems.
  30. MSPs typically purchase hardware that is energy-efficient to reduce long-term operating costs.

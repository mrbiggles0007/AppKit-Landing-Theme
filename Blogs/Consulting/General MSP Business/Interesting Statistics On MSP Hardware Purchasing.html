  1. 31% on average of an MSP budget is currently spent on Hardware Purchases.
  2. 57% of service providers will purchase from hardware vendors they have an existing relationship with.
  3. 42% of service providers prefer to purchase from a distributor over a manufacturer.
  4. 68% of service providers purchase from more than one primary server hardware vendor.
  5. 20% of service providers listed hardware purchasing as the most difficult part of their business.
  6. 56% of providers say they purchase from more than one main workstation manufacturer to reduce the chance of vendor lock in.
  7. Only 25% of providers willingly use more than one manufacturer when purchasing firewalls and networking equipment.
  8. If buying in bulk, service providers can usually obtain between 10 up to 20% discount on average.
  9. 52% of service providers consider the ability to expand via hardware upgrades as the most important feature of any hardware they want to purchase.
  10. 87% of managed service providers purchase brand new hardware exclusively.
  11. The average age of a rugged computer is 7 years versus 4 years for a standard PC.
  12. During Covid, 84% of service providers experienced significant hardware shipping delays when ordering computer hardware including PBX and VOIP equipment.
  13. 34% of service providers currently run Windows 11 as their primary desktop operating system.
  14. 46% of all MSPs run ConnectWise management software
  15. 62% of all service providers have experienced issues with retaining experienced technical staff.
  16. MSPs have the highest staff turnover rate of any industry with 13.2%
  17. 90% of service providers consider hardware automation as essential to being able to offer services (I want to know what the other 10% are on)
  18. 63% of MSPs consider themselves general purpose service providers including preferring generic hardware over specialist hardware such as Cisco.
  19. 18% of service providers considered themselves security hardware specialists (MSSP)
  20. 57% of support desk technicians worked over a holiday or weekend during the year.
  21. 32% of IT support staff had their vacation interrupted by a work request.
  22. 14% of technicians believed they had an optimum work life balance.
  23. Only 5% of service providers managed more than 15 thousand endpoints.
  24. 26% of technicians managed more than 750 hardware end points.
  25. 83% of service providers backup their clients' cloud applications this year versus 78% last year.
  26. 8% of service providers never undertake client test restores.
  27. 53% of service providers believe IT Documentation is the most important item for a PSA to integrate with.
  28. 61% intend to offer new services to current clients as their primary mode of growth in the next year.
  29. 47% of service providers believe that lack of sales and marketing is the biggest issue with obtaining new clients.
  30. 54% of service providers have added 1 to 3 new services in the past two years.

There are some surprising stats in the information above especially when it comes to purchasing hardware.

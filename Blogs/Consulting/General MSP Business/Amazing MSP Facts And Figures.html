  11. The global security-as-a-service market is expected to reach $26.5 billion by 2026.
  12. Insider threats account for 33% of all cybersecurity incidents, making user and entity behavior analytics (UEBA) a valuable emerging security service.
  13. Vulnerability management services are expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% through 2025.

  15. The global email security market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% through 2025.
  16. Zero-trust security solutions are gaining popularity, with 60% of organizations expected to adopt them by 2023.
  17. Managed security service providers can help SMBs achieve 100% compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
  18. Cyber insurance policies have become more prevalent, with MSPs offering security assessments to help clients determine their risk and potential coverage.
  19. Compliance-as-a-service (CaaS) is a growing sector, with MSPs providing regulatory compliance support for industries like healthcare and finance.
  20. Threat hunting services are becoming more popular, with MSPs using AI and machine learning to proactively identify potential threats.
  21. Managed incident response services are in demand, with 57% of organizations indicating they will outsource incident response within the next year.
  22. MSPs are offering security awareness training to clients, with an emphasis on educating employees on how to identify and avoid phishing attacks.
  23. Network security services are expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% through 2025.
  24. Managed firewall services are becoming more popular, with MSPs helping clients secure their networks from outside threats.
  25. Cybersecurity analytics and reporting services are expected to grow at a CAGR of 26.3% through 2025.

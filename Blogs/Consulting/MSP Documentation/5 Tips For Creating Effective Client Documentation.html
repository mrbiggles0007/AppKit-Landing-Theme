5 Tips On Documentation In IT Small Business Support in 2023

Published 14 days ago5 min read

Documentation is a critical aspect of Managed IT Services. Without proper documentation, an IT Solutions provider can quickly find itself struggling to maintain the high-quality service levels that small business support demands. In today's fast-paced business world, documentation is a key factor in providing top-notch IT services, especially for SMB IT Services providers. In this article, we will provide five tips on documentation in IT Small Business Support that Managed Service Providers can use to provide a better experience for their clients.

Tip 1: Choose the Right Documentation Platform

When it comes to documentation platforms, there are several options available. However, for Managed Service Providers, we recommend using IT Glue. This class-leading documentation platform is used by most Managed Service Providers, making it the go-to solution for IT support and services. IT Glue provides a centralized location for storing all of your client information, making it easy to access when you need it. Additionally, IT Glue provides integrations with popular cloud services, which can be an added benefit for small business support.

Tip 2: Implement a Standardized Documentation Process

Having a standardized documentation process is critical for any MSP. It ensures that everyone in your organization is on the same page and that documentation is created and organized consistently. One way to standardize your documentation process is by using ConnectWise Manage. This PSA application is an excellent tool for managing support desk functions, as well as projects and client information storage. It provides a centralized location for all documentation, making it easy for staff to access the information they need. Additionally, ConnectWise Manage provides reporting features, enabling you to track how documentation is being used in your organization.

Tip 3: Utilize Remote Monitoring and Management

Another critical aspect of documentation in IT Small Business Support is the use of remote monitoring and management tools. ConnectWise Automate is an RMM tool that enables you to manage remote client devices in a help desk environment. It provides features such as patch management, remote access, and scripting, making it an essential tool for MSPs. With ConnectWise Automate, you can monitor client systems in real-time, providing you with valuable information that can be used to improve your documentation.

Tip 4: Develop a Comprehensive Documentation Strategy

Developing a comprehensive documentation strategy is crucial for any MSP. It ensures that documentation is created, organized, and used effectively across your organization. At Optimized Documentation, we help Managed Service Providers develop documentation strategies that are tailored to their specific needs. We understand that every organization is unique, and we work with our clients to develop strategies that work for them.

Tip 5: Outsource Documentation Management to an Experienced Company

Once you have completed a documentation remediation project, the most critical aspect of a documentation strategy is to outsource the task of documentation management to an experienced company such as Optimized Documentation. There are several reasons why outsourcing documentation management is the right decision. Firstly, it provides continuity over a long time frame, ensuring that your documentation remains up-to-date regardless of staff movements. Secondly, it enables you to maintain standards, regardless of who is creating the documentation. Finally, it prevents reinvention of the wheel every time a new staff member starts.

Conclusion

In conclusion, documentation is a critical aspect of Managed IT Services, and a poor documentation strategy can cause massive amounts of time to be lost. By following the five tips we have provided in this article, Managed Service Providers can create a documentation strategy that will ensure they are a competitive MSP that will likely succeed. Additionally, outsourcing documentation management to an experienced company such as Optimized Documentation can provide additional benefits that cannot be achieved through an in-house approach.

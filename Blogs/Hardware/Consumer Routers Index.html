Overview Of Consumer Routers

Published 6 months ago4 min read
Consumer routers are ubiquitous devices that are commonly found in households and small offices. They serve as the gateway between a local area network (LAN) and the internet, allowing devices within the network to connect to the internet and communicate with each other.

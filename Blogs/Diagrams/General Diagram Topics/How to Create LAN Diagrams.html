Paessler: Custom Network Diagrams Made Easy

Paessler is a powerful diagram application that is used for many types of diagrams, including network diagrams. It offers a variety of templates that you can use as a starting point for your LAN diagram. Here's how to create a LAN diagram using Paessler:

  1. Open Paessler and click on "New Diagram".
  2. Choose the "Network" category, and select "Custom Network Diagram".
  3. Give your diagram a name and choose the orientation.
  4. Drag and drop devices and connections onto the canvas.
  5. Customize the appearance of the diagram by changing colors, fonts, and sizes.

Paessler also allows you to create your own custom devices and connections, giving you complete control over the appearance of your diagram. With Paessler, you can easily standardize your diagrams across all clients, saving you time and energy.

MyDraw: How to Create a Network Diagram

MyDraw is another popular diagram application that is used for many types of diagrams, including network diagrams. Here's how to create a LAN diagram using MyDraw:

  1. Open MyDraw and click on "New".
  2. Choose the "Network Diagram" category, and select "Basic Network Diagram".
  3. Give your diagram a name and choose the orientation.
  4. Drag and drop devices and connections onto the canvas.
  5. Customize the appearance of the diagram by changing colors, fonts, and sizes.

MyDraw offers a variety of built-in network shapes, icons, and symbols that you can use to create your LAN diagram. It also supports importing custom shapes and symbols from other sources. With MyDraw, you can easily create professional-looking diagrams that are consistent across all clients.

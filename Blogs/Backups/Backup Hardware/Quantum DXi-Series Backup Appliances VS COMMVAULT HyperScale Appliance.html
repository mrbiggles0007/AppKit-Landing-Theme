Quantum DXi-Series Appliance Overview

Quantum DXi-Series is a range of backup appliances that provide scalable and reliable data protection for businesses of all sizes. The appliances use patented deduplication technology to reduce the amount of data that needs to be backed up, stored, and transmitted. This reduces storage requirements, network bandwidth, and backup windows, making it an efficient and cost-effective solution for IT contract services and their clients.

NAS Features and Benefits

Quantum DXi-Series offers several features and benefits that make it a popular choice for IT contract services:

  1. Patented Deduplication Technology: Quantum DXi-Series uses patented variable-length deduplication technology that reduces the amount of data that needs to be backed up by up to 90%. This means that IT contract services can save on storage costs and reduce backup windows.
  2. Scalability: Quantum DXi-Series appliances have scalable capacity up to 216 TB and are designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes. This makes it easy for IT contract services to add capacity as needed without having to invest in new hardware.
  3. High Performance: Quantum DXi-Series appliances can backup and restore data quickly and efficiently. This ensures that IT contract services can meet their clients' recovery time objectives (RTOs) and recovery point objectives (RPOs).
  4. Integrated with Leading Backup Software: Quantum DXi-Series appliances are integrated with leading backup software, including Veeam, Commvault, Veritas, and others. This makes it easy for IT contract services to deploy and manage the appliances as part of their backup infrastructure.
  5. Encryption and Security: Quantum DXi-Series appliances provide encryption and security features that protect data at rest and in transit. This ensures that IT contract services and their clients can comply with regulatory requirements and protect against cyber threats.

Traditional Backup Methods Compared

Traditional backup methods involve backing up data to tape or disk-based systems. These methods are time-consuming, expensive, and prone to errors. Quantum DXi-Series provides a more efficient and reliable backup solution that reduces backup windows, storage requirements, and costs. It also provides faster recovery times and ensures that data is protected against cyber threats and other risks.

NAS Architecture and Scalability

The architecture of a backup appliance is an important factor to consider when selecting a solution. The Quantum DXi-Series backup appliances utilize a patented variable-length deduplication algorithm that maximizes storage efficiency while maintaining high performance.

The appliances also offer a hybrid configuration, which combines disk and tape storage, providing an additional layer of protection against data loss. The Quantum DXi-Series appliances are highly scalable, allowing IT contract services to add additional capacity and performance as needed.

The Commvault HyperScale Appliance, on the other hand, uses a distributed scale-out architecture that allows IT contract services to add compute, storage, or both independently. This architecture provides a flexible and scalable solution that can adapt to changing business needs. The HyperScale Appliance also features an all-flash array option that delivers high performance and low latency.

NAS Data Management and Protection

Effective data management and protection are important for businesses of all sizes. The Quantum DXi-Series appliances offer several features that help IT contract service organizations manage and protect their clients' data. 

For instance, the appliances include policy-based replication, which allows data to be automatically copied to remote backup locations, ensuring that critical data is protected against disasters.

The appliances also include software-defined data protection, which simplifies backup and recovery operations by automating the process of protecting data.

The Commvault HyperScale Appliance offers a range of data management and protection features, including snapshot-based backup and recovery, policy-based data migration, and archiving. 

These features provide IT contract services with a comprehensive solution that can protect their clients' data across multiple platforms and environments.

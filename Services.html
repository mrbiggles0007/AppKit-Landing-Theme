Services Provided

Our preference is to take on a full documentation upgrade project and that will always be the goal in the back of our mind, that is not to say that it is not a good idea to start out slow. Quite often we will be asked to come in and do a review of the current setup & make recommendations.

Based on that, our clients often have us do a task that standalone will be usefull to their business & depending on the outcome of that task will then be receptive to recommendations on the rest of their documentation framework.

Like anything, trust takes time to build up and we understand that. The initial reveiw and recommendations are normally good for 12 months before they likely need to be reviewed. A password naming review and plan is almost always a great first step. Once staff are used to the new system, it allows for a much faster time to access especially for new staff and we set it up in a way that is intuitive meaning there is less lead time for new staff to become comfortable finding the password they need in the least amount of time.

Take a look through the various services & if you have questions about any of them, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Fixed Flexible Asset Implementation

Part of the documentation project is to implement a consistent core flexible asset framework.

This does not include the automated flexible assets that are part of a different component of the upgrade, it relates to the static flexible assets used by your organization.

We take all of the current static flexible assets used and look for commonality across all clients and if need be, adjust our existing templates so they contain information important to your organization.

We then export the existing information in your flexible assets and manually add them to the new flexible assets that will replace them prior to importing them back into your documentation platform. The structure across all clients is identical and the information is filled out as completely as possible.

This can be a pretty time consuming task. An example of one component that needs to be completed is we have a vendor flexible asset where we tag all vendors a client uses & that links to the vendor organization. The issue is that most MSPs do not enter their vendors as organizations into their documentation platform. This can be a large job as we have to create each vendor an organization uses in IT Glue or Hudu and this involves verifying the details are correct such as full address and primary contact.

If the MSP is large then this can mean hundreds of organizations and even though we have templates for the most common, most of your clients will have a number of vendors only they use. Once in place though, new clients can have their vendor page completed within seconds & it gives the support desk a fast effective way of obtaining 3rd party details for vendors specifically pertaining to that client.

This is an example of one of 12 or so fixed flexible assets that are implemented as a consistent framework across all clients.

This would be considered a required part of the documentation upgrade project.
